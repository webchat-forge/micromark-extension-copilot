# Gaussian Integral

The Gaussian integral, also known as the probability integral:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

## Significance

This integral is fundamental in:
1. Probability theory
2. Statistics
3. Physics
4. Signal processing

## Related Forms

1. **General Form**
   \[
   \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-ax^2} dx = \sqrt{\frac{\pi}{a}}
   \]

2. **Two Dimensions**
   $$
   \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-(x^2+y^2)} dx\,dy = \pi
   $$

3. **Probability Density**
   The normalized Gaussian function:
   \[
   f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
   \]
